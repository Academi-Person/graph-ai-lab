About Graph-AI-Lab

Graph-AI-Lab is a research group dedicated to artificial intelligence, graph mining, and complex network science. The lab studies how information, influence, behavior, and structural patterns emerge and evolve in interconnected systems such as social networks, information networks, biological networks, communication systems, and knowledge graphs.


Our research combines machine learning, network analytics, graph representation learning, optimization, diffusion modeling, and AI to develop scalable and interpretable methods for real-world graph data. The main goal of the lab is to design intelligent models that can understand, analyze, and optimize complex networked systems.
